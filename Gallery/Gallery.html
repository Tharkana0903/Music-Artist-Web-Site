Justin Bieber

Justin Bieber "JB" is a is a Canadian singer and songwriter. widely recognised for his genre-melding musicianship and has played an influential role in modern-day popular music. He can also play guitar, piano, drums and trumpet.

Early Life

Justin Bieber was born on March 1, 1994 (age 28) and was raised in Stratford, Ontario, Canada. In early 2007, aged 12, Bieber sang Ne-Yo's "So Sick" for a local singing competition in Stratford and was placed second. After his mother posted a video of the performance on YouTube for their family and friends to see.

Billboard Music Awards

In May 22, 2011, "Justin Bieber" won the awards for Fan Favorite Artist, Top Social Artist, Top Streaming Artist, Top New Artist, Top Digital Media Artist and Top Pop Album for "My World 2.0" at the Billboard Music Awards at the MGM Grand Garden Arena in Las Vegas.

iHeartRadio Music Awards

Justin Bieber Takes Home Three Awards at iHeartRadio Music Awards 2016. He won the award for Male Artist of the Year, Best Fan Army for 'Belibers' and Dance Song of the Year for 'Where Are Ü Now' at the iHeartRadio Music Awards at Kia forum in Inglewood, Calif.

Grammy Awards

2016, 60th Anual grammy Award, Justin Bieber won their first-ever Grammy Award for "Where Are Ü Now" in Best Dance Recording. 2021, 63rd annual Grammy Award, he won his second Grammy award for 10,000 Hours, in the category "Best Country Duo/Group Performance".

Best hit Song

"Baby" was released as the lead single on Bieber's debut album, "My World 2.0"on January 18, 2010. The song has sold 3.9 million digital downloads in the United States and is certified Diamond in the country. The official music video is the 40th most-viewed video on YouTube. In the United States, "Baby" debuted at number five on the Billboard Hot 100.

Best hit Song

"Sorry" is a Justin Bieber fourth studio album, Purpose (2015). It was released on October 22, 2015, as the second single from the album. It spent seven weeks at number one on the Canadian Hot 100 and three weeks at number one on the US Billboard Hot 100. The song became one of the best selling digital music releases with over 10 million in sales in 2016 alone.

Best Hit Albums

"My World 2.0" is the latter of two-part debut studio album from Canadian singer Justin Bieber, and is his first full studio release,The album was released on March 19, 2010. My World 2.0 debuted No.1 in the Billboard 200 selling 283,000 copies, Justin became the youngest solo male artist to top the chart since Stevie Wonder in 1963.

JB PURPOSE WORLD TOUR

The Purpose World Tour is Bieber's third concert tour. The tour started on March 9, 2016, in Washington, and concluded on July 2, 2017, in London. Purpose World Tour grossed $163.3 million and sold 1,761,642 tickets in 2016 and the 29 shows in 2017 grossed $93.7 million with 1,043,839 tickets sold.
